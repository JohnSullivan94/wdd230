Rule of Thirds

Autozone

There is a great use of the rule of thirds on the Autozone website; in both the large and small scale displays. There is a distinct use of it between the nav bar, site links, and ads. The flow of the page also follows the natural flow of the eye which starts at top left of the screen flowing either to the bottom left or top right.

Alignment

Burger King

Burger Kings website uses the rule of alignment very well in both large and small displays. The nav bar, adds, and site links use their own contrasting alignements so as not to overuse, but they still work very well together. The alignment has a nice flow from left to right and top to bottom.

Contrast

Spotify

Spotify uses great contrast in their website. They do with great use of their products colors iplenting them into the website as a whole. The contrasts are also simple and not overone in any way. It helps the user feel comfortale with the website in all ways.
