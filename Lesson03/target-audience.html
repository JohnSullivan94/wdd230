Joe Smith

Joe Smith is 34 and owns his own plumbing and electrical company. Joe likes to check the weather to understand how his day will go as the weather does have an effect on his job. He needs to know what to prepare for in case he needs extra personal equipment to work with certain weather patterns.

Photo by Generated Photos

Pam Steeley

Pam Steeley is a 42 year old elementary school teacher. She likes to get a look at the weather as she sometimes uses it for her schooling lessons. If she can get a correct outlook at the weather she can plan for her day/weeks activities a little better.

Scenarios:

  1. What is todays weather?
  2. What is this weeks weather?
  3. What is tomorrows weather?
  4. Is there rain in the forecast?
  5. How warm/cold is it going to be?
